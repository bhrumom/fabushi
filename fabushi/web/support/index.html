About the App

关于应用

Mahayana is a Buddhist scripture reading and sharing platform that connects practitioners worldwide. Features include scripture reading with Text-to-Speech, global Dharma sharing via video and text feeds, AI-powered scripture assistant, meditation tools, and community interaction.

大乘是一款佛教经文阅读与分享平台，连接全球修行者。功能包括经文朗读（TTS）、 全球法布施视频与文字、AI 经文助手、禅修工具和社区互动。

🛡️ Zero Tolerance for Objectionable Content 不当内容零容忍政策

We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive behavior. All reported content is reviewed and acted upon within 24 hours. Users can report content and block other users directly within the app.

我们对不当内容和滥用行为实行严格的零容忍政策。所有举报内容将在 24 小时内审核处理。 用户可在应用内直接举报内容和屏蔽其他用户。

Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

How do I create an account? 如何创建账户？ ›

You can register using your email address or sign in via Alipay. Guest mode is also available for browsing content without an account.

您可以通过邮箱注册或支付宝登录。也可以使用游客模式浏览内容。

How do I report inappropriate content? 如何举报不当内容？ ›

Tap the "..." (more options) button on any content to access the Report feature. Select a reason and submit your report. We will review it within 24 hours.

点击内容上的"..."（更多操作）按钮，选择"举报内容"，选择原因并提交。我们会在 24 小时内审核。

How do I block a user? 如何屏蔽用户？ ›

Tap the "..." button on the user's content and select "Block User". Their content will be removed from your feed immediately.

点击用户内容上的"..."按钮，选择"屏蔽用户"。该用户的内容将立即从您的信息流中移除。

What content is not allowed? 哪些内容是不允许的？ ›

We prohibit pornography, violence, hate speech, harassment, spam, misinformation, and copyright infringement. Violators will be permanently banned.

禁止色情、暴力、仇恨言论、骚扰、垃圾信息、虚假信息和侵犯版权的内容。违规者将被永久封禁。

How does the AI assistant work? AI 助手如何使用？ ›

The AI assistant runs locally on your device for maximum privacy. It can help explain scripture passages and answer questions about Buddhist concepts.

AI 助手在您的设备上本地运行，最大程度保护隐私。它可以帮助解释经文段落和回答佛学问题。

Is my data secure? 我的数据安全吗？ ›

Yes. We only collect minimal information needed to provide our services. Your personal information is never sold or shared with third parties without your consent.

是的。我们只收集提供服务所需的最少信息。您的个人信息不会在未经同意的情况下出售或分享给第三方。
